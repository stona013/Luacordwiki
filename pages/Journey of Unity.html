Feature: Denial

Evolving Feature: Crystalline Awakening

As your journey continues, the crystalline presence inside you begins to manifest in tangible ways, reflecting your internal struggle and progression toward integration.

Level 3: Anger

Crystalline structures begin to grow across your body, blooming in moments of stress or fury.

  • You gain a number of Remnants equal to half your Proficiency Bonus (rounded up).
  • Whenever you deal psychic damage, you can expend one Remnant to deal an additional 1d6 + your character level psychic damage.
  • Regaining Remnants: When you spend a Psionic Energy Die or Bardic Inspiration, you regain 1 Remnant. You regain all expended Remnants after a long rest.

Level 5: Bargaining

A faint whisper gnaws at your mind, you are no longer fully yourself. Something moves with you, protecting you in ways you do not yet understand.

  • Whenever you spend your reaction, you can expend 1 Remnant to create a Psionic Push. Choose one effect:
    • Self-Shift: Move up to 30 feet without provoking opportunity attacks.
    • Telekinetic Shove: A creature within 30 feet must succeed on a Strength saving throw against your spell save DC or be pushed 15 feet in a direction of your choice. (They may choose to fail.)

Level 7: Depression

You begin to accept that you are no longer who you once were. The crystals may not be fully part of you, but you now wield them with understanding and intent.

  • When you use a bonus action on your turn, you can expend 2 Remnants to create a Psionic Static. Choose one effect:
    • You and all creatures connected by your Telepathic Bond gain advantage on the next roll they make.
    • All enemies within 30 feet must succeed on a Charisma saving throw or suffer disadvantage on their next roll.

Level 10: Acceptance

Two have become one. Your mind, once fractured, now channels harmony through the crystalline soul that shares your body.

  • You now gain a number of Remnants equal to 1 + half your proficiency bonus (rounded up).
  • When using Psionic Push or Psionic Static, you can now double all applicable ranges.

By now you found a way to channel your Duality into a state of Harmony which you can use to help or harm. On your turn if you spend your action, you can also expend three Remnants to invoke your full state of being, for 1 minute you gain the following benefits:

Harmony State
Activation: Action + 3 Remnants
Duration: 1 minute
While active:

  • You may concentrate on two spells at once. If concentration is broken on one, both end.
  • If you make a weapon attack, you can cast a spell as a bonus action, and vice versa.
  • You treat psychic damage resistances as none, and immunities as resistances.
