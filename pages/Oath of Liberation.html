Oath of Liberation

Paladins who swear the Oath of Liberation answer a single command: no bond is eternal, no wall unassailable. They are siegebreakers in shining mail, wielding divine power not to conquer but to pry open every prison, whether wrought of iron bars, tyrannical decrees, or crippling fear. In their creed, mercy is offered to the shackled, never to the jailer. Deliberation yields to decisive action, for hesitation grants oppressors time to tighten their grip. Where a Liberator walks, locks fail, chains turn to dust, and the first gasp of freedom spreads through the crowd like fire along dry wheat. Kingdoms welcome them as heroes, until those same realms forget that laws can also be cages.

Tenets of Liberation:

Break Every Chain:
No unlawful bond, physical, magical, or political deserves to stand.

Expose the Stronghold:
Fortifications that shelter tyranny must be breached so others may judge what lies within.

Strike Without Delay:
Hesitation grants oppressors time to tighten their grip. Act the moment injustice appears.

Channel Divinity

At 3rd level you gain two options; you can use only one Channel Divinity per short or long rest.

Chainbreaker

When you or a creature you can see within 30 ft has or gains the grappled, restrained, paralyzed, stunned, petrified, charmed, or frightened condition (or is bound by mundane restraints), you can use your reaction and expend your Channel Divinity. Choose one such effect on that creature; the effect ends immediately and non-magical bonds crumble. You can activate Chainbreaker even while you are the affected target, bypassing the usual action restrictions of those conditions.

7th-Level Feature

Aura of Release

Your oath radiates a field that slackens chains and opens lanes where none existed.

Freedom Aura
While you are conscious, you and friendly creatures within 10 feet benefit from all three effects below:

  • You have advantage on saving throws and ability checks made to resist, end, or avoid the following: grappled, restrained, frightened, charmed, stunned, petrified, any forced movement that would move you against your will.
  • Non-magical difficult terrain inside the aura costs no extra movement.
  • A friendly creature that begins its turn in the aura does not provoke opportunity attacks that turn.
