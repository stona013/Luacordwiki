Way of the Inkheart

Core Concept & Flavor:

The Way of the Inkheart is a monastic tradition that believes the act of creation is the purest form of enlightenment. Followers of this path learn to infuse their artwork with Ki, manifesting living calligraphy and brushwork onto reality. These monks can conjure ephemeral constructs, ethereal weapons, and healing symbols through masterful strokes.

They carry enchanted brushes, often made of the whiskers of ancient spirits or feathered quills carved from celestial beings, and draw using special ki-infused ink that clings to the air itself.
