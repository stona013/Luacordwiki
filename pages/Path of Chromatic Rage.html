Path of Chromatic Rage

Some Dragons inspire their followers to pitch themselves into a ferocious battle fury with Destruction. These barbarians are Dragon warriors who channel their rage into a powerful displays of Chromatic Energy

A variety of dragons in the worlds of Lua inspire their followers to take this path. Many dragons in Xendevaar are looking for followers which can prepare them for this path, so that they can become the strongest weapons for their plan. Even in the 4th level of hell, there are fiends and devils who make a pact with dragons to take this path.

Voice of the Dragon

10th-level Path of Chromatic Rage feature
At this stage, your connection to dragons deepens, allowing you to channel their terrifying roars in battle. With a single shout, you strike fear into the hearts of your enemies, force them to focus their attacks on you, or unleash a blast of elemental fury. In these moments, you are a true avatar of dragonkind, your voice shaking the battlefield.

At 10th level, you can unleash a powerful dragon roar as a bonus action. Choose one of the following effects:

  • Frightful Roar: Each creature of your choice within 30 feet must make a Wisdom saving throw (DC = 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Constitution modifier) or become frightened of you. A creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of its turn, ending the effect on a success.
  • Taunting Roar: Each creature of your choice within 30 feet must make a Wisdom saving throw (DC = 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Constitution modifier). On a failed save, the creature has disadvantage on attack rolls against any target other than you until the end of its next turn.
  • Furious Roar: Each creature in a 30-foot cone must make a Dexterity saving throw (DC = 8 + your proficiency bonus + your Constitution modifier). On a failed save, a creature takes 3d8 damage of the type associated with your scale color. On a success, the creature takes half damage.

You can use this feature a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus, and you regain all expended uses after finishing a long rest.
